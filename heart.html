What is Heart Disease?

Heart disease refers to various types of conditions that affect the heart's structure and function. It is one of the leading causes of death globally. Common types include coronary artery disease, heart failure, and arrhythmias.

Common Symptoms

  • Chest pain or discomfort
  • Shortness of breath
  • Fatigue
  • Irregular heartbeat
  • Swelling in legs, ankles, or feet

Prevention Tips

  • Maintain a healthy diet
  • Exercise regularly
  • Avoid tobacco use
  • Manage stress
  • Regular health checkups
